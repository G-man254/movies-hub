13 Hours: Benghazi

Duration: 1hr 31mins

13 Hours: Benghazi is a 2016 American action thriller film directed by Michael Bay and written by Chris Terrio and Chuck Logan. The film is based on the 201 book of the same name by Mitchell Zuckoff, which tells the story of the 201 attack on the American diplomatic compound in Benghazi, Libya.
